## Why Blockchain Matters

Blockchain technology is revolutionizing:
- **Finance** - Decentralized financial systems
- **Supply Chain** - Transparent tracking
- **Identity** - Self-sovereign identity
- **Gaming** - True ownership of in-game assets
- **Voting** - Transparent and verifiable elections
- **Real Estate** - Fractional ownership
- **Healthcare** - Secure medical records
